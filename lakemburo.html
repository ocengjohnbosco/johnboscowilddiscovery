    1 DAY LAKE MBURO NPK WILDLIFE SAFARI-TOUR (From $120pp)

    One Day Lake Mburo NPK Wildlife Safari will take you to Lake Mburo National Park found in western Uganda where you will have an Experience of big numbers of savanna wildlife. Explore more of the wildlife species like Zebras, warthogs, Impalas and also a treat of a boat cruise offers more of the aquatic wildlife like Crocodiles, Hipos together with so many Bird species.

    View Safari Highlights:

    •Early Morning Breakfast
    •Transfer to Lake Mburo NPK
    •Park Game Drive
    •Boat cruise on Lake Mburo
    •Return to Kampala/Entebbe

    DAY Tour :Detailed Itinerary Lake MBURO NATIONAL PARK

    This Day Lake Mburo tour is topped up with a pick up from your residence [Home,lodge or Hotel] by our professional safari guide/driver after your early morning breakfast. And you will be transferred to Lake Mburo National Park, key to note interesting but among the smallest National parks in Uganda. On your arrival, we check into the park and begin our game drive right away as goes for about 3 hours, while enjoying our drive, you will have wonderful views of many wildlife species including; the newly introduced Giraffes, Zebras, Buffaloes, Leopards, Topis, Mpalas, and many different bird species. After lunch at any lodge with in the park, we prepare for an afternoon boat cruise on the lake Mburo, while on the boat cruise there are bigger chances of seeing different animal species including; the Hippos, Topis, Zebras Crocodiles, Buffaloes and many other water birds along the shores of the lake

    After the boat cruise, you shall leave the park and proceed back to Kampala/Entebbe or to your residence, it will be a great drive as there will be a stopover at the equator, here you will have chance to take some photos and a polar water experiments. Another stop will be at the local place where drums are made commonly known as Mpambiire Village, then continue with your journey arriving Kampala in the late evening.
