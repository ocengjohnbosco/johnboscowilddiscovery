    OUR SAFARIS

    Photography and Filming Safaris .

    We put our expertise and years of Conservation experience into action to provide host and logistical support for photography and film crews. working on location in East Africa. We can help make life easier for your team from start to finish, assisting with permits, transport, local contacts and more. Filming can be challenging in most of these areas for a number of reasons. We can help you with that. Let us handle planning and logistics for your trip so you can stay focused on your technical and creative work.

    Study Tours, Volunteering and Internship Safaris. Interested in hands-on volunteer experiences working with animals or communities? We can organize internships and volunteer programs for clients who with the aim of inspiring conservation action to save wildlife species, and a better understanding of local cultural, social and environmental issues. We also organize student study tours around students’ interests, including forest ecology, Savannah ecology, and other themes that highlight Uganda’s magnificent National Parks and Reserves

    Ecological and Environmental Research Safaris Promoting ecological research, and related conservation and environmental projects are one of our major activities aimed at contributing to the conservation of nature, for sustainable tourism industry and development. We, therefore, provide logistical support to research crews wishing to carry out activities in Uganda

    The Wild Discoveries Partnership Trust . The Wild Discoveries Partnership Trust (WPT) is an autonomous non-profit unit dedicated to conservation in regions where we operate tours. The aim of the WPT is to enhance community livelihoods and long-term conservation of East Africa’s natural resources

    The WPT achieves its objectives through special projects that fall into two categories; Conservation Science and Community Empowerment. The Trust focuses on projects that benefit the Earth and people by prioritising ecosystem services, combating climate change, and sustainable development. Wild Discoveries Safaris (U) Ltd contributes a voluntary donation to the WPT from every safari trip.
