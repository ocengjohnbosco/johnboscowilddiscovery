    1 Day Gorilla Tracking Tour | Rwanda

    1 Day Gorilla Tracking Tour Rwanda

    Day Gorilla Trek Experience Itinerary

    The safari starts and end in Kigali the capital of Rwanda. Our driver/Safari Guide will pick you from your Hotel or Lodge (after having breakfast or with packed breakfast) at around 4:30 am to start on your 116km journey to Volcanoes National park in the western part of Rwanda.

    The journey to Volcanoes national park takes between 2-3 hours on average driving speed and on reaching the park headquarters, you will be briefed by park officers and then assigned to a gorilla group to trek. You will then be led into the forest by a ranger guide to track and meet the Mountain Gorillas.

    The Gorilla trek exercise may take between 30 mins to 8 hours depending on the movements of the Gorillas. However, upon locating the gorillas, you will then spend 1hr with them. Enjoy the unique adventure and you can take pictures (with no flash).

    After the 1hr which most times seems to be like minutes, then we trek back out of the forest and then transfer back to Kigali for your flight back or a stay in Kigali.
