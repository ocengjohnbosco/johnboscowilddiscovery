    About Wild Discoveries Safaris Uganda

    Wild Discoveries Safaris is a registered Ugandan ecotourism and conservation company. We are committed to promoting responsible ecotourism that benefits conservation and communities. Safaris can be so much more than touring places, taking photos and purchasing souvenirs. Travel can promote positive change for the Earth and for people. Wild Discoveries Safaris helps you make a difference while on holiday

    Our experienced guides are equipped with modern vehicles and incredible knowledge of the local area to give our clients the best possible chance to see some of nature’s greatest animals. Having operated for nearly a decade we know that being reliable, trustworthy and contactable are key to your successful holiday and we put this at the core of our company values.


    Our Vision
    Wild Discoveries Safaris’ vision is to be a leader in responsible tourism and conservation practices that transform local livelihoods, enhance conservation of nature, and achieve sustainable tourism development.

    Our Mission
    At Wild Discoveries Safaris, we strive to provide outstanding service to our clients, while enhancing local livelihoods and promoting the understanding, appreciation and conservation of nature, culture and the environment. We take utmost consideration of our clients’ interests and concerns and promote honesty and transparency

    Our Values
    Our core values are the basis for establishing a common understanding between Wild Discoveries Safaris and our clients and giving back to communities. Our Core Values are: 1. Integrity, 2. Sustainability and 3. Creativity.
